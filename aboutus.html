From Passion to Excellence

In 1995, Wheel Hub Founded by a team of automotive enthusiasts, our journey began with a small showroom and a big dream. From the very beginning, we set out to create more than just a dealership--we aimed to build a community united by a shared passion for all things automotive. Driven by our commitment to excellence, we quickly gained recognition for our uncompromising standards and meticulous attention to detail. Our showrooms, strategically located across the United States, soon became synonymous with quality cars and automotive accessories.

OUR VISION

Leading Automotive Retailer

At Wheel Hub, we envision ourselves as the foremost automotive retailer, setting the standard both online and in-store. Our goal is to be the premier choice for car buyers, offering a trustworthy and convenient shopping experience that exceeds expectations. We are committed to continuous innovation, constantly refining our services to stay ahead of the curve and meet the evolving needs of our customers. Moreover, we aspire to cultivate a vibrant community of passionate car enthusiasts, fostering connections and sharing our love for all things automotive.

Our mission is to empower drivers with a seamless and satisfying automotive shopping experience. From product browsing to payment completion, we ensure that every interaction with us is a seamless, convenient and empowering experience.
